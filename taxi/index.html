Taxi Apps in Japan
GO Taxi JA only
Available across Japan. You can book by specifying pick-up and drop-off on the map. Credit card pre-payment and in-app receipts are supported. The app interface is in Japanese only, so a translation app may help.
NearMe Airport EN support
Shared rides from your location to major airports. You can split costs with other passengers. Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before departure. Credit card payment only.
Uber EN support
Available in Tokyo and other major cities. Download the app, register your account and payment method, then enter your destination. The app matches you with nearby taxis.
