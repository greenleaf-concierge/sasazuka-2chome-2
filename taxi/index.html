Let’s explore some of the transportation options available in Japan:

让我们探讨一下日本的一些交通方式：

{Go タクシー}

Coverage: Go Taxi operates across all 44 prefectures in Japan (as of August 2023) and serves a wide range of areas.

Features: You can easily reserve and book a taxi by specifying your pick-up and drop-off locations directly on the map. Registration is straightforward, requiring basic personal information such as your name and email address. For online payments, you can also add your credit card details. Additionally, Go Taxi allows for online pre-payment during your ride, and you can even request receipts through the app.

Language Support: Note that Go Taxi’s app interface is available in Japanese only, so a translation app may be necessary.

覆盖范围：Go Taxi 的业务遍及日本所有 44 个都道府县（截至 2023 年 8 月），服务范围广泛。

特点：您可以直接在地图上指定上车和下车地点，轻松预订出租车。 注册很简单，需要基本的个人信息，例如您的姓名和电子邮件地址。 对于在线付款，您还可以添加信用卡详细信息。 此外，Go Taxi 允许您在乘车期间在线预付款，您甚至可以通过该应用程序索取收据。

语言支持：请注意，Go Taxi 的应用程序界面仅提供日语版本，因此可能需要翻译应用程序。

{NearMe Airport}

Description: NearMe Airport combines the convenience of taxis with the flexibility of buses. You can book shared rides from specified locations to major airports without waiting for fixed schedules or designated stops. Plus, you can share the ride and split costs with fellow passengers. Even if you use highways, there are no additional charges. The ability to cancel for free up to 48 hours before departure is another advantage.Language Support: NearMe Airport provides English support, but online payments are accepted via credit cards only.

{靠近我的机场}描述：NearMe Airport 结合了出租车的便利性和巴士的灵活性。 您可以预订从指定地点到主要机场的拼车服务，无需等待固定的时间表或指定的站点。 此外，您还可以与其他乘客共乘并分摊费用。 即使您使用高速公路，也无需支付额外费用。 另一个优势是可以在出发前 48 小时内免费取消。语言支持：NearMe Airport 提供英语支持，但仅接受通过信用卡进行在线支付。

{Uber}

Overview: Uber is a taxi dispatch service available in over 700 cities worldwide. In Japan, Uber collaborates exclusively with existing taxi companies due to legal restrictions.Coverage: Uber operates in cities like Tokyo, Yokohama, Sendai, Nagoya, Osaka, Kyoto, and Fukuoka. For specific details, visit their website.How It Works: After downloading the app, create an account and register your email or phone number. If you plan to use online payments, add your credit card information. When you need a ride, input your destination and pick-up location. The app will match you with nearby taxis, and you can track their location on the map while waiting.

{优步}概述：Uber 是一项出租车调度服务，覆盖全球 700 多个城市。 在日本，由于法律限制，优步仅与现有出租车公司合作。覆盖范围：优步在东京、横滨、仙台、名古屋、大阪、京都和福冈等城市运营。 有关具体详情，请访问他们的网站。工作原理：下载应用程序后，创建一个帐户并注册您的电子邮件或电话号码。 如果您打算使用在线付款，请添加您的信用卡信息。 当您需要乘车时，输入您的目的地和上车地点。 该应用程序将为您匹配附近的出租车，您可以在等待时在地图上跟踪他们的位置。
